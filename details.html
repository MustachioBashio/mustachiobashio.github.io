2018 Details

Mark your calendars for April 21, 2018!

In late April, head on down to W. 6th Street in Downtown Cleveland for the mustachiest bar crawl for charity!

The crawl will start at Barley House at 6pm. Pick up or purchase your shirt when you get in to get drink specials all night long!

At 10pm, we will move to the second bar, Dive Bar, right down the street for the dance party to end all dance parties.

Barley House

Barley House is proud to call the Cleveland Warehouse District home.

Located at 1261 W. 6th St, Cleveland, OH 44113, Barley House is a short distance from both East 4th and West 9th.

With your official Mustachio Bashio shirt, you can get special prices on both alcohol and food.

We will be at Barley House from 6pm until 10pm.

The following events will be taking place at Barley House:

  • Karaoke
  • Mustache Judging
  • Awards Presentation

Dive Bar

With 42 beers on tap, Dive Bar will undoubtedly please you.

Located downstairs at 1214 W. 6th, Cleveland, OH 44113, Dive Bar is essentially right across the street from Barley House.

We will be at Dive Bar from 10pm until 2am.

The following events will be taking place at Dive Bar:

  • Champions Table with champagne toast
  • Dance Party with DJ
